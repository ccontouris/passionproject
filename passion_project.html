Birds I'v Seen in Chicago!!

This is a webpage dedicated to the birds I've seen in Chicago!! Sometimes with pictures I took!!

Why birds?? Birds are as much a part of our everday enviroment as tress and grass. They are everywhere living amoung us. When I stop and look at the birds I'm reminded of the bustling ecosystem constantly adapting to survive and thrive, much like us! Birds are great indicators of the health of our enviorment as they are resilent and adaptable. Once the birds start going you can be sure ecocolgocial collapse is emminent!! So stop and take a gander at the birds every once in awhile I think you'll be amazed at what you observe!!

A bit about the birding community: Chiacgo has amazing birding communinties that focus on inclusion. Everything from BIPOC bird clubs, feminist bird clubs, as well as anti-war advocacy the birding community is filled with people from all walks of life standing in unison with one another. The birding community is deeply engrained in enviormental justice we work together to improve the enviroment for all living creatures. We are currently organzing to petetion the city of Chicago for lights out nights during migrations to protect our birds on their long voyage. Interested in joining the community?
